Monster Survivors

Game Features

  • • Endless survival gameplay
  • • Multiple character abilities
  • • Progressive difficulty system
  • • Dynamic monster spawning

How to Play

  • • Use WASD or arrow keys to move
  • • Automatically attack nearby enemies
  • • Collect experience to level up
  • • Choose new abilities as you progress

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where you face endless waves of increasingly challenging monsters. As you progress, you'll unlock powerful abilities and upgrades that help you survive longer against the growing hordes.

Each run is unique, offering different ability combinations and strategies to explore. How long can you survive against the monster onslaught? Test your skills and see if you can beat your previous records!
